Filing a Roof Claim Shouldn't Be a Second Job

Dealing with insurance after storm damage is stressful, confusing, and time-consuming. Most homeowners don't know what their policy covers, what documentation the adjuster needs, or how to push back when a claim is underpaid or denied.

That's where we come in. Salty Dawgz Roofing has extensive experience working with every major insurance carrier in Michigan. We know what adjusters look for, how to document damage properly, and how to make sure you get the coverage you're paying for.

How We Handle Your Insurance Claim

1

Free Storm Damage Inspection

We get on your roof, inspect every component, and identify all storm-related damage — including damage you can't see from the ground.

2

Professional Documentation

We compile a detailed damage report with close-up photos, measurements, and descriptions that match the format insurance companies expect.

3

File Your Claim

We help you file the claim correctly and on time. We'll walk you through exactly what to say and what documentation to submit.

4

Meet With Your Adjuster

We meet your insurance adjuster on-site, walk the roof together, and make sure nothing is missed or undervalued in their assessment.

5

Roof Repair or Replacement

Once your claim is approved, we complete the work to the highest standard — and handle all the final paperwork with your carrier.

What You Should Know

  • You pay nothing for the inspection or documentation
  • We work with all major insurance carriers
  • Claims have time limits — don't wait to file
  • We advocate for you, not the insurance company
  • If your claim is underpaid, we help you supplement
  • Your only out-of-pocket cost is your deductible
  • We never pressure you — it's your home, your decision

Common Insurance Claim Mistakes

Many homeowners unknowingly hurt their own claims. Waiting too long to file, not documenting damage immediately, doing temporary repairs without photos first, or accepting the first adjuster estimate without question can all reduce your payout. We make sure none of that happens.
