Your Roof Is Only Part of the Picture

A great roof needs a complete exterior system to fully protect your home. Gutters channel water away from your foundation, and siding shields your walls from moisture, wind, and temperature extremes. When any of these fail, the damage compounds fast — especially in Michigan.

Salty Dawgz Roofing installs and replaces gutters and siding alongside our roofing work, giving you a single trusted contractor for your entire home exterior.

Gutter Services

✓

Seamless Gutter Installation

Custom-fabricated on-site to fit your home perfectly. No seams means no leaks. Available in aluminum and copper with a wide range of colors.

✓

Gutter Guards

Stop cleaning gutters forever. We install high-quality gutter protection that keeps leaves, pine needles, and debris out while letting water flow freely.

✓

Gutter Repair & Replacement

Sagging, pulling away, overflowing, or leaking at the seams? We repair or replace damaged sections to restore proper drainage.

✓

Downspout Extensions

We make sure water is directed far enough from your foundation to prevent basement flooding and erosion — critical in Michigan's heavy rain seasons.

Why Bundle With Your Roof?

When you're already getting a new roof, it's the perfect time to address gutters and siding. The crew is already on-site, scaffolding is up, and we can ensure everything integrates properly — drip edge to gutter, flashing to siding. Bundling also saves you money compared to separate projects.
