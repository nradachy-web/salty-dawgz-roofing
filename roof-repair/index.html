Not Every Roof Needs Replacing

A lot of roofing companies will push a full replacement the second they see an issue. We don't. If your roof can be properly repaired and still give you years of reliable protection, that's exactly what we'll recommend. We're honest about what you actually need.

That said, a "cheap fix" that doesn't address the root cause will cost you more in the long run. We take the time to trace leaks to their true source, assess surrounding damage, and repair it in a way that lasts — not just a patch that buys a few months.

Common Roof Repairs We Handle

✓

Leak Detection & Repair

Water stains on your ceiling don't always mean the leak is directly above. We trace every leak to its actual entry point and fix it at the source.

✓

Missing or Damaged Shingles

Wind, age, and storms can strip shingles off your roof. We match your existing shingles as closely as possible for a seamless repair.

✓

Flashing Repair

Damaged or poorly installed flashing around chimneys, vents, and skylights is one of the most common leak sources. We reseal and replace as needed.

✓

Vent Pipe Boot Replacement

Rubber pipe boots crack and deteriorate over time, letting water drip straight into your attic. We replace them with quality boots built to last.

✓

Ice Dam Damage

Michigan winters are brutal on roofs. Ice dams can force water under your shingles and into your home. We repair the damage and help prevent future issues.

Why Repairs Matter in Michigan

Oakland County gets hit with heavy snow, ice storms, high winds, and intense summer heat. Small roof damage that goes unfixed becomes big damage fast — water infiltration leads to rotting decking, mold growth, and insulation damage that costs thousands more to fix later.

Catching problems early with a proper repair saves you money and extends the life of your roof significantly.
