When Is It Time for a New Roof?

Your roof is the single most important line of defense between your family and Michigan's brutal weather. When shingles start curling, granules clog your gutters, or leaks keep coming back despite repairs — it's time for a full replacement, not another patch job.

At Salty Dawgz Roofing, we specialize in complete residential roof replacements across Oakland County. We strip your old roof down to the deck, inspect the wood sheathing for rot or damage, replace anything compromised, and install a brand-new roof system that's engineered to last decades.

Our Roof Replacement Process

1

Free Roof Inspection

We climb up, photograph everything, and assess the full condition of your roof — no charge, no obligation. If your roof still has life left, we'll tell you.

2

Detailed Written Estimate

You get a clear breakdown of material options (architectural, designer, flat), labor, timeline, and total cost. No hidden fees, no surprises.

3

Complete Tear-Off & Install

We strip the old roof, inspect and repair the decking, install ice & water shield, new underlayment, drip edge, and your chosen shingles — all to code.

4

Final Walkthrough & Cleanup

We run magnets for nails, clean every gutter, haul away all debris, and walk through everything with you. Your yard is left spotless.
