FAQ

Questions? We've Got Answers.

How much does a new roof cost in Oakland County?

It depends on size, pitch, material, and any decking repairs. We provide a free inspection and detailed written estimate so you know the exact cost before any work starts. No guessing, no surprises.

Do you help with insurance claims?

Absolutely. We handle the entire process — from the initial damage inspection and photo documentation to meeting with your adjuster on-site. We fight to make sure you get every dollar you're owed.

How long does a roof replacement take?

Most residential roofs are completed in 1-2 days, weather permitting. We'll give you a clear timeline before the project starts and keep you updated throughout.

What roofing materials do you use?

We work with top-tier manufacturers and offer architectural shingles, designer shingles, and flat roofing systems. We'll recommend the best option for your home's style, budget, and Michigan's climate.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes — fully licensed, bonded, and insured in the state of Michigan. We carry general liability and workers' compensation so you're completely protected.

Do you offer financing?

Yes. We offer flexible financing options so you can protect your home now and pay over time. Ask us about available plans when you get your estimate.

What if I just need a small repair, not a full replacement?

We do repairs of all sizes — from a few missing shingles to flashing fixes to leak tracing. If a repair is all you need, that's what we'll recommend. We don't upsell.
