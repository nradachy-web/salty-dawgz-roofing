Michigan Storms Hit Hard — We Respond Fast

Oakland County sees severe thunderstorms, hail, high winds, heavy snow, and ice storms throughout the year. When a storm hits your neighborhood, the damage to your roof might not always be visible from the ground — but it can be serious enough to cause leaks, structural issues, and thousands of dollars in interior damage if left unaddressed.

At Salty Dawgz Roofing, we specialize in storm damage response. We'll be on your roof quickly, assess every inch, document the damage with detailed photos, and guide you through the next steps — whether that's a targeted repair or a full insurance-covered replacement.

Types of Storm Damage We Handle

⚡

Hail Damage

Hail can bruise, crack, and dislodge shingles — compromising your roof's waterproofing even when it looks fine from the ground. We know exactly what to look for.

⚡

Wind Damage

High winds lift, crease, and tear shingles — exposing the underlayment and decking to water intrusion. We repair or replace affected areas before leaks start.

⚡

Fallen Tree & Debris Damage

Branches and trees can puncture your roof, damage the structure, and create emergency situations. We provide emergency tarping and permanent repairs.

⚡

Ice Dam Damage

Michigan winters create ice dams that force water under shingles and into your home. We repair the resulting damage and improve ventilation to prevent recurrence.

Don't Wait After a Storm

Insurance companies have time limits on when you can file a claim for storm damage. The longer you wait, the harder it becomes to prove the damage was storm-related and the more secondary damage (leaks, mold, rot) accumulates. Call us immediately after a major storm — we'll inspect your roof for free and help you understand your options.
